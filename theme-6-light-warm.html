Features

Cloud Management, Reimagined

No more clicking through consoles or memorizing CLI commands. Just describe your intent.

Natural Language Commands

Just type "install nginx" or "check disk space" - the AI understands your intent and executes the right commands.

Multi-System Control

Select multiple servers and run commands across all of them simultaneously. Use @mentions for quick targeting.

Instant Provisioning

Spin up new servers on AWS or Hetzner with a simple request. No more clicking through cloud consoles.

AI-Powered Insights

Get human-readable summaries of command outputs. AI interprets logs, metrics, and errors for you.

Secure by Design

Agent-based architecture with secure WebSocket connections. Your credentials never leave your servers.

Real-Time Updates

Watch command execution in real-time. See output as it happens, not after it's done.
