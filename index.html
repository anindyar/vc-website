How It Works

Three Simple Steps

From natural language to executed commands in seconds.

1

Connect Your Fleet

Install our lightweight agent with a single command. Works on any Linux server — cloud, on-prem, or bare metal.

2

Describe Your Intent

Type what you want in plain English. "Check disk space", "Deploy nginx", "Show me what's using CPU".

3

AI Executes & Explains

Platform

Built for Real Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade security and reliability without the complexity.

Natural Language Commands

Just describe what you want. AI understands context and executes the right commands across your fleet.

Fleet-Wide Control

Run commands across your entire fleet. Group by tags, regions, or custom filters. Cloud and on-prem together.

LLM Data Obfuscation

Use powerful public LLMs safely. IPs, hostnames, and secrets are masked before leaving your network.

Easy Onboarding

Connect servers with a one-liner agent install. No complex setup, no configuration files.

Zero-Trust Architecture

Real-Time Output

Watch command execution live. Stream output as it happens, not after it's done.
