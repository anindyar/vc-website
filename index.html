Features

Fleet Management, Reimagined

One interface for all your servers. Cloud VMs, on-prem racks, or bare metal — just describe what you want.

Natural Language Commands

Just type "install nginx" or "check disk space" - the AI understands your intent and executes the right commands.

Fleet-Wide Control

Run commands across your entire fleet — cloud and on-prem servers together. Group by tags, regions, or custom filters.

Easy Onboarding

Connect existing servers with a one-liner, or provision new cloud VMs instantly. Your entire fleet in minutes.

AI-Powered Insights

Get human-readable summaries of command outputs. AI interprets logs, metrics, and errors for you.

Secure by Design

Agent-based architecture with secure WebSocket connections. Your credentials never leave your servers.

Real-Time Updates

Watch command execution in real-time. See output as it happens, not after it's done.
